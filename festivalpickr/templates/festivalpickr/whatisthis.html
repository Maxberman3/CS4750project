Festival Picker

Syncing with your spotify to make your life a little easier

With music festival tickets getting more expensive each year, its important to get your money's worth. Although there is plenty of different factors that go into picking a festival each summer, naturally it's a MUSIC festival, so you want to see as many of your favorite bands as you can with one ticket. That's where we come in.

FestivalPickr syncs with your Spotify account and tells you which festivals have the most of your favorite artists in their respective lineups. You can search by simply "score"- the number of matching bands in the lineup, as well as by "value"- the number of matching bands per dollar spent on general admission. You can also specify specific weights for each artist (maybe there's that one act that's really important for you to catch this summer), and restrict by distance. Cause maybe you'd really love to do Coachella, but Cali is just a bit too far.0

Finally, if you don't have Spotify, you can always input your favorite artists manually, but you should probably get Spotify. If for no other reason than cause it's awesome.

About Us

"Loop through Lists, not Line-Ups".

We decided on that slogan while contemplating what exactly we wanted to accomplish while working on this project. It's common knowledge that it takes a lot of time and work to browse through the selection of artists and bands performing at different venues, whereas looping through a Python list can be almost instantaneous. It's our goal to provide this service-- to help to remove the work from of deciding which musical event to attend, and to just ensure that the individual only has fun throughout.

We're three third-year UVA students--Max Berman, Peter Ahn, and Nick Sarris--whom have experienced such difficulties in our daily lives, and are taking it upon ourselves to solve these problems through Python and Django. It is our goal to provide this working platform in which everyone is able to simply enjoy the process of seeking out festivals while doing away with the majority of the work involved.

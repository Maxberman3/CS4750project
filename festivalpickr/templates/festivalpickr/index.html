Upgrading your concert experience

FestivalPickr works to streamline your process of buying tickets by syncing with your Spofity account to rank the events that you would be most interested in attending.

Streamlined

With music festival tickets getting more and more expensive each year, it's important to get your money's worth. You want to see as many of your favorite bands as you can with a single ticket.

Integrated

FestivalPickr syncs with your Spotify account in order to figure out which upcoming festivals and concerts have the greatest number of your favorite artists in their respective line-ups.

Innovative

The platform implements an extensive scoring system that not only takes into consideration the number of similar bands in the line-up, but also the value of the ticket, distance, and individual weights for each artist based on your Spotify replayability.

Manual Input

If you don't happen to have Spotify, We have included functionality where you're able to input your favorite artists manually.
